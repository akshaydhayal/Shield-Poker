Current state (after join)
Phase: PreFlop

Both players have paid buy-in

Next steps
Step 1: Set deck seed (optional, for randomness)
Purpose: Set a seed for card shuffling (ideally from MagicBlock VRF)
Who: Either player
Function: setDeckSeed(gameId, seed)
UI: Not shown; add a button or do it automatically
Step 2: Deal cards
Purpose: Deal 2 cards to each player (encrypted via PER)
Who: Either player
Function: dealCards(gameId, player1Hand, player2Hand)
Note: Cards are private (encrypted in PER)
UI: Not shown; add a button or handle automatically
Step 3: PreFlop betting round
Player 1 (small blind) acts first
Actions:
Fold: Exit the hand
Check: Pass (only if no bet)
Call: Match the big blind
Bet: Raise (minimum = big blind)
After Player 1 acts, Player 2 (big blind) acts
Continue until both players have equal chips committed or one folds
Step 4: Advance to Flop
When: Both players have equal chips committed
Who: Either player can call advancePhase()
Result:
Phase changes to Flop
3 board cards are revealed (currently placeholder)
current_turn resets to Player 1
Step 5: Flop betting round
Same actions as PreFlop
Continue until chips are equal or someone folds
Step 6: Advance to Turn
When: Chips are equal after Flop
Result:
Phase changes to Turn
4th board card revealed
current_turn resets to Player 1
Step 7: Turn betting round
Same actions as before
Step 8: Advance to River
When: Chips are equal after Turn
Result:
Phase changes to River
5th board card revealed
current_turn resets to Player 1
Step 9: River betting round
Final betting round
Step 10: Advance to Showdown
When: Chips are equal after River
Result:
Phase changes to Showdown
current_turn set to None
Step 11: Resolve game
Purpose: Determine winner and distribute pot
Who: Either player
Function: resolveGame(gameId, winner)
Result:
Phase changes to Finished
Winner receives the pot
Game ends
